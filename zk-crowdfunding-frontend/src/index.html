About contribution limits

Due to technical constraints in the ZK computation, individual contributions are limited to approximately 2,147 tokens with 6 decimal places of precision. The minimum contribution is 0.000001 tokens. Larger contributions can be made through multiple smaller transactions.

How Verification Works

Our platform uses zero-knowledge proofs to ensure:

  • Your contribution amount remains private
  • The total calculation is cryptographically verified
  • You can confirm your contribution was included
  • Multiple independent parties validate the computation
